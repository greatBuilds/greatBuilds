About Us

Great Builds Engineering Services Pvt. Ltd. is a civil engineering consulting firm based in Pyuthan District, Nepal. We provide reliable, pragmatic, and cost-effective solutions across structural design, project management, and field surveying, helping clients deliver safe and sustainable infrastructure.

Our Services

Structural Design

Design of residential, commercial, and infrastructure projects with a focus on safety, standards, and efficiency.

Project Management

Planning, scheduling, cost control, and supervision to deliver projects on time and within budget.

Surveying & Mapping

Topographic surveys, route surveys, and site assessments to inform accurate design and construction.

Geotechnical Coordination

Soil investigation coordination and recommendations to support robust foundation design.

Retrofitting & Rehabilitation

Assessment and strengthening strategies for existing structures to improve resilience.

Construction Support

Site inspections, quality checks, and shop drawing reviews during execution.
